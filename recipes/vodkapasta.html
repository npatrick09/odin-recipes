Pasta Alla Vodka

Home

Servings: 2-3

Prep time: 5 min

Cook time: 25 min

Description

Craving a comforting bowl of pasta without a long ingredient list or hours in the kitchen? This simple Pasta alla Vodka recipe delivers rich, creamy flavor with just a handful of pantry staples. The smooth tomato-vodka sauce comes together in under 30 minutes, making it perfect for a cozy weeknight dinner or an impressive yet effortless meal for guests.

With a touch of garlic, a splash of vodka, and a swirl of cream, this dish transforms basic ingredients into something luxurious. Whether topped with Parmesan or enjoyed as-is, it’s a satisfying and elegant classic that proves you don’t need a complicated recipe to make something truly delicious.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz pasta (penne or any short pasta)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil or butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional, for heat)
  • 1/4 cup vodka
  • 1 cup tomato sauce (or crushed tomatoes)
  • 1/3 cup heavy cream
  • Salt to taste
  • Grated Parmesan (optional, for serving)

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil and cook the pasta according to the package directions until al dente. Reserve about 1/2 cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. While the pasta cooks, heat the olive oil or butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add the garlic and red pepper flakes (if using) and sauté for about 1 minute, just until fragrant.
  3. Pour in the vodka and let it simmer for 2–3 minutes, allowing the alcohol to cook off slightly.
  4. Add the tomato sauce to the pan and stir to combine. Let it simmer gently for 5 minutes.
  5. Reduce the heat to low, stir in the heavy cream, and season with a pinch of salt. Simmer for another 2 minutes, until the sauce is smooth and slightly thickened.
  6. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss to coat. If the sauce is too thick, add a splash of the reserved pasta water.
  7. Serve hot, topped with grated Parmesan if desired.
